About Sherlock

William Sherlock Scott Holmes, a London based detective is the world's top most detective ever. He was the first to create a profession for himself and was known all over the world for his immense intelligence in tacling crime cases. Holmes was known to be born with extraodrinary thinking capabilities and was a unique personality since childhood. He had very less friends, infact only one friend who was his flatmate too, John Watson. Holmes handled crime cases with Watson and Greg Lestrade of New Scotland Yard. Holmes was often inclined to deal very unusual crime cases, which never make him bored. He relies on Nicotine to activate his brain and is known to have been engaged in illegal drugs like cocaine in the past.

Sherlock possessed an incredibile ability of noticing and drawing deductions from the most intricate and complexly trivial details. This was his best weapon in solving crimes. Also a great thinker and observer and maintains a database of important clues of all his cases he investigates in his mind palace. Being considered by people as a high functioning socio path, he is often disliked and distrusted by the police. They beleive he is an anti-social person.

Early Life

 

From the writings of "His Last Bow", it can be estimated that he was born in 1854. He was born in London and stories do not mention any details about his parents other than his brother Mycroft Holmes. His brother was a government officer and was introduced to the reader in "The adventure of the Greek".


Sherlock began to develop his detective methods since he was an undergraduate. He handled his first cases for his fellow university students.

His wit and intelectual skills in solving the cases makes his a unique and the most intelligent face on the times.
